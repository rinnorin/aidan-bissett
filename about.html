Aidan Bissett

About

Singer known for his singles "Communication" and "Dumped." He built his career on his aiden.bisset TikTok account, which has accrued 430,000 fans. After going viral on the platform, he was signed by Capitol Records. He formed his first band, Offshore, when he was 15.

He got his first electric guitar at the age of 7. He later attended Jesuit High School in Tampa.

Football was his other passion, and he explored a career in sports as well. One of his most viewed music videos is for his single "Dumped." It has earned over 700,000 views on YouTube.

He and his family moved from the Tampa area to Oregon when he was five.

On July 27, 2021, he posted a TikTok video set to "Over The Top" by Smiley and Drake.
